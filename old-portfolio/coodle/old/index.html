Welcome to the Coodle Family Planner

The Coodle family planner is a free application that allows busy families to organise their lives. Coodle allows you to add events into a calendar and set reminders for when a task is due to schedule and plan your time. You can also create shared shopping lists or to-do-lists with other family members that can be updated by family members to save you time and plan ahead. Coodle is the only place where you can keep your life organised, on the go, all in one place.

Calendar

The calendar allows you to add events, add information about an event and add a finish date, so that you can be reminded of when a task is due to be completed.

Shopping Lists

The shopping lists allow you to create a list of items and share them with the rest of the family. Family members can edit shopping lists with suggestions.

How-to videos

How to videos allow you to search through a list of videos to find tutorials about everyday jobs around the house to speed up these tedious jobs and free up your time.

Calendar

The calendar allows you to plan and organise your day by allowing you to schedule events to a certain date, add information about an event and add a finish date, so that you can be reminded of when a task is due to be completed. It also incorporates to-do-lists so you know when you jobs need completing to help you plan your time effectively.

Shopping Lists

The shopping lists allow you to create a list of items to be bought on the next shopping trip to plan your time effectively and speed-up the process of adding everyones suggestions to a list. Shopping lists can be shared with the rest of the family allowing them to add their suggestions to the list.

How-to Videos

How to videos allow you to search through a list of videos to find tutorials about everyday jobs around the house to speed up these tedious jobs and give you more free time. Videos are simple and easy to follow and are relevant to all members of the family.

School Timetable

The school timetable allows parents to schedule their time and prepare their children by allowing them to add their childrens timetables to the ... family planner. This allows parents to know what classes their children have scheduled so they can keep on top of when P.E kits are needed and when homework is due etc.
